Today is a day to be reasonable with yourself.

Take an easy breath. Close your eyes. Picture all the things you'd like to complete for work today. Set up a little gallery in you mind's eye.

Now relax. With your eyes still closed, walk in between these scenes like you are walking through statues in a park. Wander a little. See all the elements of work that make up your life. Let your eyes rest on your top goal for the day. Just over there... that one thing you'd really like to achieve. Now stop. See it in full colour. Confirm your choice. Picture yourself completing this one goal. That is your aim for today.

Now open your eyes. As you take another deep breath, your mind gallery will evaporate into the sunlight. You may write your work goal down on a piece of paper and keep it in sight.

Henry Ford said, "obstacles are those frightful things you see when you take your eyes off your goal". So today, nothing will stand between you and that one goal. Make a promise to yourself, no matter what else comes and goes, that will be achieved.

This day will come and go, that much is certain. 

Making it count? Well, that's up to you.

You ready? Breathe in.......... Focus!
