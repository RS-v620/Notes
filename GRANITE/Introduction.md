#GRANITE

**Name** : **GRANITE**
**Full Name** : **Geological Rock Analysis & Numerical Inspection Tracking Engine**
Available Alternative :  **QUARRY**( _Quantitative Unified Analysis of Rock & Resource Yield_ )


GRANITE is a system that monitors the mine-load in crushers, trucks and identifies oversized rocks, clogged crushers and provides comprehensive analysis of the system.
