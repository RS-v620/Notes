Here in Fabulous, there's a reason you take habits slowly. Long lasting change happens through tiny steps repeated regularly over time.

Think about how you strengthen a muscle. Going from ten to a hundred pounds in one session would result in injury from which you would need to heal. But take it from ten to twelve, create a healthy plateau before moving on, and you can keep going without the backlash of friction.

Pause for a moment to see how you feel about this. Be honest with yourself.
Is there frustration that you can't get there faster?

Breathe in. Let the answers come to you.

Why the urge to transform in an instant? Is it punishment for not having succeeded in the past? Breathe in again. Do you sense any fear you won't make it?

Here in Fabulous. you are valid and valuable each step of the way. There is no conditionality to your deserving kindness and compassion. And even celebration. In fact, you are urged to celebrate every step of the way. Each action, no matter how tiny, is a full victory. It is not the action that is the triumph. It is that you accomplished it. It is you that is triumphant. 

As you move through your habits, you will enjoy the results, to be sure, but more importantly, you will realize what you are truly capable of.

The Sufi poet Rumi wrote:

*When someone is  counting out gold for you, don't look at your hands or the gold. Look at the giver.*

And so Fabulous traveler, look up. It is you that is the giver. You are the giver of change.
