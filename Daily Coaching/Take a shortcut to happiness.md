Close your eyes and take a deep breath. Find any tension in your body and stretch it gently away. As you open your eyes, look around you for an imprint of nature in your home. Is there a wood grain with its wavy lines? Or the touch of cotton threads in some fabric?

Professor Brenē Brown wrote, "I don't have to chase extraordinary moments to find happiness - it's right in front of me if I'm paying attention and practicing gratitude."

Days along your Fabulous path begin with a sense of gratitude. Realize that wood grain you see was once part of a tree. Try and imagine the shape of that very branch as it grew. That cloth you can feel stemmed from cotton plants in little puffs of white. Your breakfast came from nature. Think of the origin of everything around you. You are part of this vast world.

Know that you are connected to millions of others in the world of Fabulous. They may be in a different time zone, but on the Fabulous landscape, their path is not so very far away from yours. There are lines of gratitude that criss-cross the globe at this very moment. 

Close your eyes again and take a deep breath. Think of something you feel very lucky for. Let gratitude cascade throughout your body. Take that feeling with you for the rest of the day.
