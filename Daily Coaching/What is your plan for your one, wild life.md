In her poem The Summer Day, Mary Oliver ends with these two line :
"*Tell me, what is it you plan to do
with your one wild precious life?*"
As your habits are fortified each day, dedicate a few moments to pondering her question. No rush to answer, just invite the question. Gently

These mountains you are traveling in Fabulous, they are all connected. These steps you are taking will add up and create your entire journey. You are on a much larger quest than each habit might seem. But know that they are not isolated.

This is one great tapestry you are weaving. It is one stitch at a time, but know it is wonderous and beautiful thing you are creating. And what is more - that only you could create.

We want to know what you plan to do with your one wild and precious life. What is more we want to see that unfold. The mountains ahead contain passages for just that purpose. Keep your quest and the path will reveal itself.
