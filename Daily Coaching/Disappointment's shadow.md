Think back to the last time you felt frustrated with yourself. Was there a voice of negative self-talk you heard inside your own head? 

There is a secret in that voice and, it is time that secret was revealed. 

Martin Luther King once wrote, "there can be no deep disappointment where there is not deep love".

If you feel down on yourselves, might that just mean you believe in yourself?

Look at those disappointments of yours. Bring your frustrations out of the shadows. Shine a really bright light on them here in the Fabulous world, where it is really safe to look at them.

One is never upset that pets don't speak French. They never could, so there is no frustration to feel. If you knew you weren't capable you might not be disappointed at all. 

But maybe in truth, we know what we are made of. We know what we deserve. 

Maybe the deep disappointment only exists because there is deep love, and belief, and strength that is hiding. Maybe that frustration is proof that you know you can do it. All those hopes of yours.

Now is the time to galvanize that negative energy and befriend it. Mark its good intention, but don't let it run down an endless spiral. Redirect next time, remind it of the path you're already on here in Fabulous. Let that despair feel some relief that you are tending to those hopes. 

Maybe that's what it wanted all along.
